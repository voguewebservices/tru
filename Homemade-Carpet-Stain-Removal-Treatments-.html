Ink Stains


Ink stains can wreck havoc on the fibres of your carpeting and can possibly ruin carpet if they're not cleaned thoroughly. In several ways, you may remove the ink stains from the carpet however, most of them involve lots of elbow work. The main technique is to dip a piece of white cloth in rubbing alcohol and then dabbing the cloth over the ink stain. You should never scrub because this could cause the ink spot to spread.
